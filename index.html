Blackjack Rules & Help

Objective

Beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values

  • 2-10: Face value
  • Jack, Queen, King: 10 points each
  • Ace: 11 points, or 1 point if 11 would cause a bust

Game Flow

  1. Place your bet by clicking on chips
  2. Click "Deal" to start the round
  3. Choose your action: Hit, Stand, Double Down, or Split
  4. Dealer plays according to fixed rules (must hit until 17 or higher)
  5. Winner is determined by comparing hand values

Actions

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: End your turn with current cards
  • Double Down: Double your bet, take one more card, then stand
  • Split: If your two cards have the same value, split them into two hands, doubling your bet

Special Rules

  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2
  • Dealer must hit until 17 or higher
  • Tie ("Push"): Your bet is returned
